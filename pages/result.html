Diagnosis: **Cassava Mosaic Disease (CMD)**

**Confidence Level:** High (99%)

**Cause/Context:** This is a viral disease transmitted by the whitefly. Symptoms include distorted, mosaic-patterned, and yellowed leaves, severely impacting yield.

Recommended Treatment Plan

  1. **Immediate Uprooting:** Remove and destroy all infected plants to prevent spread via whiteflies.
  2. **Resistant Varieties:** Replant only with certified CMD-resistant cassava stems.
  3. **Whitefly Control:** Apply appropriate insecticides in the initial planting stages to manage the vector population.
